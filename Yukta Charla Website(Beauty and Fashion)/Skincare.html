Top 5 Tips for Glowing Skin

  1. Drink plenty of water.
  2. Use sunscreen everyday.
  3. Apply face pack twice a week.
  4. Moisturize every morning and night.
  5. Try double cleansing nightly.

5 Skin care tips for Oily Skin

  1. Use oil-free skincare products.
  2. Cleanse your face twice a day.
  3. Wear sunscreen outdoors.
  4. Apply moistuizer daily.
  5. Don't sleep in your makeup.
